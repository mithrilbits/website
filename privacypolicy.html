CONVERTICA PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Mithrilbits, Ltd Co ("Licensor," "we," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information collected from users ("you" or "users") of the Convertica software application ("App").

By using the App, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Policy, do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We may use analytics providers to collect anonymous usage data, such as the screens you view, the features you use, and the time and date of your interactions with the App. No personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or transmitted. Analytics providers may collect certain personal information, such as your device type and IP address, but this information is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the anonymous usage data we collect from analytics providers to analyze usage patterns and improve the performance and functionality of the App.

3. Data Protection and Security

We take data protection seriously and use reasonable measures to protect the security and confidentiality of your information. We protect your information by not collecting, storing, or having access to personal information to begin with, as all the information collected is anonymized.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may use analytics providers to collect anonymous usage data. We do not disclose your information to any third parties other than analytics providers.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the following choices and rights with respect to your information:

  • You may opt out of data collection by analytics providers by using Apple's App Tracking Transparency settings. To do so:
    1. Go to your device's Settings
    2. Scroll down and tap on the Convertica app
    3. Tap on "Tracking"
    4. Toggle the switch to the off position. If the toggle switch is already off or not available, you are already opted out.

    Please note that opting out of data collection may limit your ability to use certain features of the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by users of all ages. However, we encourage parents and legal guardians to monitor and supervise their children's use of the App. If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe that your child's personal information has been collected by our analytics providers without your consent, please contact us at privacy@mithrilbits.com. We will work with you to resolve the issue with the analytics provider and have the collected personal information removed.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time by posting a revised version on our website or within the App. Your continued use of the App after the effective date of the revised Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns about this Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@mithrilbits.com.

Effective Date

This Policy is effective as of the date shown at the top of this page and applies to all information collected from users of the App on or after that date.
